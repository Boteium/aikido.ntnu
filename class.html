合氣道簡介

About Aikido

合氣道，源自於日本，是1920年代由道祖植芝盛平所創立的一門武道。合氣道根源於大東流合氣柔術，為柔術的一支。內容上，包含體術、劍術與杖術。體術方面，則包含關節技與摔技。

Aikido is a budo founded by Ueshiba Morihei, the Doshu, in 1920s in Japan. It's a branch of Jujitsu originates from Daito-Ryu Aiki-jujitsu. Aikido training focuses on both physical training and weapons. The primary weapons are ken and jo. Physical training is mainly composed of joint manipulations and throwing techniques.



合氣道的特點為以柔克剛，借力使力。透過破壞對手重心，引導對手的力量，以避開攻擊，或是進一步控制對手。比之於力量強弱，更重視技巧有無，因此適合所有年齡、體格、性別的學習者。

Aikido emphasizes on unbalancing the opponenet and the redirection of momentum in order to evade offence or to pin down the attacker with joint lock. In other words, skill, in some ways, is taken more seriously than physical strength. Because of the emplasis on effectiveness, strength won't be the limiting factor for beginners. Therefore, Aikido is suitable for all generations, genders, and ages.


社課內容

Class Information

體術方面，初學以護身、合氣道八大技法為主。學習歷程分為六級，分級如下：初級／級外（白帶）、五級（黃帶）、四級（紅帶）、三級（綠帶）、二級（藍帶）、一級（咖啡帶）。

Beginners will start with Ukemi and 8 basic techniques. There are six grades for kyu students in the following order. Rokyu (white belt), Gokyu (yellow belt), Yonkyu (red belt), Sankyu (green belt), Nikyu (blue belt), Ikkyu (coffee belt)



※ 每學期末舉行晉級考評量學習情況，審核晉級與否。一級之後，需通過晉段測驗方能晉升初段（黑帶）
※ Exams for kyu-grade students are held at the end of each semester.
　 Exam for promotion to shodan for ikkyu students is held by local Aikido association, which
　 would not take place in NTNU.
